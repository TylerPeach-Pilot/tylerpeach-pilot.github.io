By accessing the Services, you acknowledge reading and agreeing to this Privacy Policy. For questions, contact info@peachpilot.ai.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Peach Pilot collects information you voluntarily provide when submitting contact forms, booking discovery calls, applying for employment, corresponding by email, or entering contractual relationships. This includes name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When visiting the Site, Peach Pilot and third-party providers automatically collect technical information including: IP address and approximate geographic location; browser type, version, and operating system; pages viewed, time spent on pages, navigation paths, referral URLs, and exit pages; device identifiers; and usage data. This collection occurs through cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6).

1.3 Client and Enterprise Data

For clients using the enterprise AI platform, Peach Pilot processes data as a data processor, including business data, documents, emails, communications, workflow and process data, and user account information. Processing follows the applicable Master Services Agreement and Data Processing Agreement.

1.4 Information from Third Parties

Peach Pilot may receive information from third-party sources including business contact databases, professional networks (e.g., LinkedIn), analytics providers, advertising partners, referral sources, and business partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide and Improve Our Services

Peach Pilot uses information to: operate, maintain, and improve the Site and Services; respond to inquiries and provide customer support; process applications and onboard clients; deploy and manage AI agents and workflows; and monitor and analyze usage patterns.

2.2 Communications

The company sends administrative and transactional communications (account notices, service updates), responds to questions and correspondence, and sends marketing and promotional communications where permitted by law and subject to opt-out rights.

2.3 Legal and Compliance

Peach Pilot uses information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, enforce its Terms of Service, protect rights and property, and detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

2.4 Business Operations

Information is used to evaluate hiring and recruitment processes, conduct research and analytics, and support corporate transactions such as mergers, acquisitions, or financing.

3. How We Share Your Information

Peach Pilot states: “We do not sell your personal information.” Information may be shared in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

Peach Pilot shares information with third-party vendors and service providers including cloud infrastructure (Microsoft Azure), AI model providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google), analytics providers, and communication tools. These providers are contractually obligated to use information only for services provided and in accordance with the Privacy Policy.

3.2 Affiliates and Related Entities

Information may be shared with parent companies and affiliates, including Hive Financial Assets, for business operations, service delivery, and compliance purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

Peach Pilot may disclose information if required by law or if it reasonably believes disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests; protect rights or property; prevent or investigate wrongdoing; or protect personal safety.

3.4 Business Transfers

If Peach Pilot is involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or corporate transaction, information may be transferred as part of that transaction. The company will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on the Site prior to your information becoming subject to a different privacy policy.

3.5 With Your Consent

Peach Pilot may share information for other purposes with explicit consent.

4. Data Retention

Peach Pilot retains personal information as long as necessary to fulfill purposes described in the Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by applicable law. Specifically:

  • Website visitor and contact data: retained for up to 3 years from last interaction.
  • Client and enterprise data: retained in accordance with the applicable Data Processing Agreement and client instructions.
  • Employment application data: retained for up to 2 years from the date of application.
  • Legal and compliance records: retained as required by applicable law.

When personal information is no longer needed, the company will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with data retention and destruction policies.

5. Data Security

Peach Pilot implements administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Security practices include:

  • Hosting on Microsoft Azure with enterprise-grade security controls.
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication requirements.
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring.
  • Employee training on data handling and security practices.
  • Vendor management and third-party security requirements.

The company notes: “No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.” While Peach Pilot strives to protect information, it cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your information has been compromised, contact info@peachpilot.ai immediately.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and collect usage data.

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function; cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help understand how visitors interact with the Site (e.g., Google Analytics); data is used in aggregate form to improve the Site.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences and settings to improve user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant content and track marketing effectiveness; set only with consent where required by law.

6.2 Managing Cookies

Users can control cookies through browser settings. Most browsers allow refusing cookies, deleting existing cookies, or receiving notification when cookies are set. Disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality. Where required by applicable law (including GDPR), Peach Pilot will request consent before placing non-essential cookies.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have certain rights regarding personal information.

7.1 All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of personal information held.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Objection: Object to processing in certain circumstances.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing communications using the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting info@peachpilot.ai.

7.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of collected personal information, subject to exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: Peach Pilot does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: The company does not use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide Services.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Peach Pilot will not discriminate for exercising CCPA rights.

To exercise California rights, contact info@peachpilot.ai. Peach Pilot will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days as required by law.

7.3 European Users (GDPR)

If located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access, rectification, and erasure.
  • Right to restriction of processing.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a local supervisory authority.

Lawful bases for processing personal information include: performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, legitimate interests, and consent. For questions about legal bases or to exercise GDPR rights, contact info@peachpilot.ai.

8. International Data Transfers

Peach Pilot is headquartered in the United States. If accessing Services from outside the U.S., be aware that information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction.

For transfers of personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to the United States, Peach Pilot relies on appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, where applicable.

If Peach Pilot learns it has inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 16, it will promptly delete it. If you believe information from a child under 16 has been collected, contact info@peachpilot.ai.
