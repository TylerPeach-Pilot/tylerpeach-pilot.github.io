Before Right Quote existed.

The case that built this company.

A 72 year old man who'd had something like a stroke seven years back. The application went in anyway. Weeks passed. The carrier wanted more, then more again. Then the decline. Ten hours on the phone, nothing earned, and a man who never got covered because starting over felt impossible.

In Right Quote, that case is three extra questions on the first call: what happened, how long ago, what he takes for it. The verdict shows up carrier by carrier before anyone applies. The no takes minutes, not weeks. And when it says yes for the answers he gave, that yes comes from the carrier's own rulebook.

A real story from the founding team. It's why Right Quote exists.
